Upper Limb Pain


ER

If: Arm, shoulder or back pain that comes on suddenly, is unusually severe, or is accompanied by pressure, fullness or squeezing in your chest (this may signal a heart attack)


Urgent Care or Primary Care

If: Severe pain and swelling in your arm trouble moving your arm normally or turning your arm from palm up to palm down and vice versa



Upper Limb Injury


ER

If: obvious deformity or protruding bone in your arm or wrist, especially if you have bleeding or other injuries


Urgent Care or Primary Care

sudden injury to your arm, particularly if you hear a snap or cracking sound.
