Male Groin Pain



ER

If: severe pain (and swelling) in your testicles that happens very quickly, groin pain plus fever or blood in your urine, a painful groin swelling plus nausea and vomiting .


Urgent Care or Primary Care

If: pain doesn't improve in a few days, you have pain in the testicles that lasts for longer than a few days, there's a lump or swelling in a testicle



Female Groin Pain



ER

If: excessive vaginal bleeding with palpitation, if patient is pregnant and is also experiencing some heavy vaginal bleeding just go to the ER right away.


Urgent Care, Primary Care or Gynecologist

If: Pain during sex, vaginal itching and discharge.


NOTE: PLEASE DON'T GO TO THE EMERGENCY ROOM FOR A ROUTINE STD TESTING.
