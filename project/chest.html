Chest Pain



ER

If: this is the worst pain of your life and radiates to your left jaw and left shoulder, you're experiencing chest pressure with shortness of breath and dizziness. have history of any heart condition


Urgent Care or Primary Care

If: you have known history of GERD or Acid Reflux.


Chest Trauma


ER

If: sudden feeling of SOB and obvious deformity of the chest


Urgent Care or Primary Care

If: more than 3 days from the date of injury with no breathing complications but with some bruising and pain at the site of trauma.
